How Visually Impaired Are Facing Challenges

Statistics

The estimated number of people visually impaired in the world stands at 285 million, with 39 million diagnosed blind. Asia alone accounts for 63.4% and 57.5% of worldwide visual impairment (VI) and blindness, respectively.
According to the World Blind Union, “more than 90% of all published material is not accessible to the blind or partially sighted.”
