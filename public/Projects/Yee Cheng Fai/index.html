Dealing with Autism

In today's society, normal people treat autistic people differently and this should not be the case. There is a need for us to treat them equally.

Find out how

Challenges faced by autistic people

Challenge 1

Difficulty in verbal or non-verbal communications

Challenge 2

Aggressive behaviour

Challenge 3

Stimming and/or self-injurious behaviour

Challenge 4

Rigid routines and resistance to change

How WE can treat autistic people normally

BE PATIENT

Be sure to talk about or reward good behavior often. Be generous with compliments for good behavior.

Running around and playing outside may be a better way of sharing time together. it will also let them relax and feel calmer.

INTERACT THROUGH PHYSICAL ACTIVITY

BE PERSISTENT BUT RESILIENT

Don't let your feelings get hurt if they do not respond to you as you'd like. While their responses may be blunt, they do not mean it.

Be sure to talk about or reward good behaviour often. Be generous with compliments for good behaviour.

ALWAYS STAY POSITIVE
