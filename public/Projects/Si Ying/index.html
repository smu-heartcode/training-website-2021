You are not alone!

There are many other people who are experiencing retrenchment too, but fret not! Government is providing recovery grants to retrenched workers to help them overcome this difficult time.

There will be some information on the recovery grant provided by Government below.

Recovery Grant

The COVID-19 Recovery Grant (CRG) provides temporary financial support of $700/mth for 3 months to workers in lower- to middle-income households who are currently experiencing job loss.

For more information, click here.

SkillsFuture

There are four key areas of focus under the SkillsFuture Council:

  1. Helping individuals make well-informed choices in education, training and careers.
  2. Developing an integrated, high-quality system of education and training that responds to constantly evolving industry needs.
  3. Promoting employer recognition and career development based on skills and mastery.
  4. Fostering a culture that supports and celebrates lifelong learning.

For more information, click here.

LinkedIn

Afraid of not being able to find job? No worries! LinkedIn is the world's largest professional network on the internet. You can pick up new skills that can be essential for your future job(s) and look for your suitable job or internship on LinkedIn.

For more information, click here.

e2i

e2i serves as a bridge between workers and employers, connecting with workers to offer job security through job-matching, career guidance and skills upgrading services, and partnering employers to address their manpower needs through recruitment, training and job redesign solutions.

For more information, click here.
