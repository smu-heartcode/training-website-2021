Myth and Facts about Autism

Myth#1


People with autism do not make eye contact.

Fact#1

They will make eye contact when they feel comfortabe with the person .

Myth#2

People with autism cannot talk.

Fact#2

Based on the spectrum,the level of commuication differs.

Myth#3

Individuals on the autism spectrum do not have feelings and thus are unable to show affection.

Fact#3

The display of affection may appear different from typical people.
