Stories

There is always a rainbow after the storm , here are some personal stories of how people overcame their struggles, so don't give up just yet !

Add Your Survival Story !
Bottling up feelings :

I struggle to open up to family, friends and pretty much anyone else in my life. i dont tell anyone things i think they can use against me. and it is so draining. recently i started work and my parents are trying to get me to open up more to them, i feel forced to but ultimately its helping me build my confidence around other people. i love working and talking to people. it helps my anxiety, its like i can talk to complete strangers fine but not people i know. its weird. my advice is to try and open up. dont bottle up feelings because they can eat you up inside.

Bullying:

In primary school I was bullied for 3 years which was most of primary school. It got to the point where I didn't like life but then I built up the courage to stand up for myself and it all got better and luckily, I got to have the best last two years of school with my friend. The moral of the story : to stand up for yourself.

My Mother :

My mother died on Mothers' Day. I realised that it's ok to cry. It's ok to miss her but you have to keep moving forward. I had responsibilities to look after like my sisters and brothers , and my niece and nephew. Even though she was my life, my moon , my stars , my life , I had to let her go. I see her in the flowers outside, in my family , in myself. I will miss her but I have friends and family to talk to when I feel down. So in conclusion, if you're upset then talk to people. It does help. xox
